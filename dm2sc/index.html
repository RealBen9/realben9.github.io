In this new rendition of the epic saga of Dogeminer, you travel to the moon, mars and even jupiter in search for mountains of dogecoins.

With shiny pickaxes and legendary fortunes, you will increase your luck, loot find, wow and more.

Soon, the DIA (Doge Intelligence Agency) job will be available, where you must travel backwards in time, with a very important mission.

Waste no time, start mining now! The sky is the limit, and it is no limit for Doge.
