Usage

What is DSUM & DRANGE?

DSUM & DRANGE collectively measure the "rarity" of the words in a seed.

Each word is assigned a DSCORE based on it's percentage given in the Google Ngram Viewer (for 2019), relative to every word in the answer list. A higher DSCORE means the word is less common.

DSUM is the average of each correct word's DSCORE in the seed. DRANGE is the range of DSCOREs in the seed, so a lower DRANGE means a more consistent "rarity".

Viewing Seeds

Viewing a seed will display the correct words for that seed.

Step 1 : Enter a seed into the "Seed" input

Step 2 : Select the mode

Step 3 : Press "View Seed!"

Finding Seeds

When a seed is found, only the seed, DSUM & DRANGE values are shown.

Step 1 : Select the mode

Step 2 : Enter the amount of seeds you want into the "Seeds" input. Note that finding more seeds will take longer.

Step 3 : Enter the highest DSUM value you want to play into the "DSUM High" input. Note that finding seeds closer to a DSUM or DRANGE of 2315 or 0 will take longer.

Step 4 : Enter the lowest DSUM value you want to play into the "DSUM Low" input. Note that finding seeds within a smaller High-Low range will take longer.

Step 5 : Enter the highest DRANGE value you want to play into the "DRANGE High" input.

Step 6 : Enter the lowest DRANGE value you want to play into the "DRANGE Low" input.

Step 7 : Press "Seedfind!"
