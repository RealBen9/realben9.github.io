Keys

How to get them

Firstly, to get here you did 1 of 3 things.

A : You found part of a key. | B : You viewed the repository. | C : You manually typed this URL in.

If you did A, you know what they look like. Otherwise, this is how you get them :

At the bottom-right corner of the screen, there may be part of (there are 8 parts) a key. (you may need to explore the website first.)

Instead of the word EXAMPLE, they will have a number, followed by 3 bytes.

After you have found part of a key, you can either remember it (although there are several parts of a key), or write it down.

What they do

Firstly, let's assume we have all 8 parts.

[EXAMPLE]

Next, there are 3 steps.

Step 1 : Go to the main page.

Step 2 : Add ?key= to the URL.

Step 3 : Add all 8 parts in order to the new URL.

If you have found all of them, (and typed everything out correctly,) this should lead to a secret page.
